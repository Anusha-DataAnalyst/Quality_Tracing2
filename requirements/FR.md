# Functional Requirements

## FR-01
Patients shall be able to request appointments.

## FR-02
Patients shall view prescriptions.

## FR-03
Patients shall view office visit history.

## FR-04
Users shall log in to the system.

## FR-05
System shall redirect users based on role.

## FR-06
Admins shall manage users.

## FR-07
Admins shall manage hospitals.

## FR-08
HCP shall document office visits.

## FR-09
HCP shall view patient records.

## FR-10
Patients shall edit demographics.
