# Non-Functional Requirements

## NFR-01
System shall enforce role-based access control.

## NFR-02
System shall require authentication for all protected resources.

## NFR-03
System shall maintain secure sessions.

## NFR-04
System shall log system actions.

## NFR-05
System shall ensure least privilege access.

## NFR-06
System shall maintain data integrity.

## NFR-07
System shall be maintainable via modular architecture.

## NFR-08
System shall be reliable via testing.

## NFR-09
System shall ensure availability of healthcare services.

## NFR-10
System shall perform efficiently under normal load.
